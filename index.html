WHO WE ARE

Here's a little bit about us.

VISION.

FundUs is an upcoming idea which connects altruists and social workers, who would like to bring change to a society by working on impacting projects for example making water accessible to a village by digging a borewell; but are struggling to get funds. Essentially, it is a kickstarter for social workers.

FUNCTIONALITY

Social workers post their ideas and give an estimate on how much it is estimated to cost. Altruists browse through the list of projects and choose the project that is in line with their values and fund it. The funding could be ranging from as small as $5 (a small meal price) or hefty large sums.

SUPPORT

Altruists are encouraged to donate more by getting rewards. Social workers reliability should be rate based on their execution and effectiveness on their earlier projects.
